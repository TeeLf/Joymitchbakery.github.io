Meet Joy

Baked with heart, every single day.

Bake and Bloom began with a simple belief: that a perfectly made cake — crafted with real butter, fresh eggs and genuine care — can make any ordinary day feel special.

Chef Joy Mitchell trained at a professional patisserie before bringing her craft home with one clear mission: bake three cakes perfectly, every single day. No shortcuts. No additives. Just blooming sweetness in every tin.
